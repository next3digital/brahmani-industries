Our Manufacturing Process

Precision engineered components through advanced CNC technology and rigorous quality control

1

Raw Material Selection

We meticulously source high-quality metal alloys from trusted suppliers to ensure exceptional durability, strength, and optimal machinability for all our components.

2

CNC Machining

Our state-of-the-art CNC turning centers deliver micron-level precision with tight tolerances (±0.01mm), producing components with flawless surface finishes and perfect geometries.

3

Threading & Finishing

Advanced thread cutting and precision deburring processes create perfectly formed threads while surface treatments enhance corrosion resistance and aesthetic appeal.

4

Quality Inspection

Every component undergoes rigorous inspection using calibrated gauges, CMMs, and visual checks to verify dimensional accuracy and material specifications.

5

Cleaning & Sorting

Components undergo ultrasonic cleaning and meticulous sorting processes to ensure absolute cleanliness and proper organization before packaging.

6

Packaging & Dispatch

Products are securely packed in customized packaging with proper cushioning, labeled with full traceability, and dispatched with reliable logistics partners.
